About Me

Who Am I?

Hi I'm Zhoudan (Zoey) Xie. I recently completed my Ph.D. in Economics from the George Washington University. My research interests include macroeconomics, regulation, innovation, firm dynamics, and economic growth. I specialize in applying advanced natural language processing (NLP) techniques and text-as-data analysis to study the economic effects of regulation.

I'm currently a Financial Economist at the Public Company Accounting Oversight Board (PCAOB). Prior to this role, I worked as a Senior Policy Analyst at the GW Regulatory Studies Center.

Citizenship: U.S. Citizen
